Ben's Important Locations

Map 1:

My first map is centered on Portland, Oregon. Portland is important to me because it is the closest "big city" to where I grew up. It was only about an hour drive north, so my family spent a lot of time there when we needed to shop somewhere! I also have lots of family here.

Map 2:

Next up is Silverton, my home town. Here is where I went to high school and met most of my friends. It also happens to be halfway between Portland and Eugene! Silverton is small but beautiful, especially Silver Falls.

Map 3:

Finally we have Eugene. This is where I attend college and have lived for the past 4 years. I have so many great memories in Eugene and have made so many great friends. I hope to live close whenever I end up finding a job.
